About Us

Discover our restaurant story

Our thoughtfully designed interiors create an inviting atmosphere where every detail reflects our commitment to elegance and comfort. Whether you're seeking an intimate dinner, a celebratory feast, or a gathering of friends, BlackBox provides the perfect backdrop.

At BlackBox, we take pride in sourcing the finest local ingredients, giving you a taste of Guntur's culinary essence. Our menu takes you on a global journey, blending international influences with regional authenticity.
